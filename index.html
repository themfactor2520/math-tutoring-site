About Us

The M Factor is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization that provides free online math tutoring specifically for kids from under-resourced communities who lack access to private academic support. Our mission is to equip these students with the confidence and skills they need to succeed in math through accessible, high-quality instruction.

Our Tutoring Program

  • Free 1-on-1 or group sessions via Zoom or Google Meet
  • Qualified volunteer tutors from high school and college
  • Aligned with school curriculum and personalized to student needs
